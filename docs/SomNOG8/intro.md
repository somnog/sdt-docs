## **SomNOG8 Track 3: Software Development (MERN Stack)**

**Track 3: Software Development** will focus on **Full-Stack Web Development with the MERN Stack** (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js). This hands-on workshop is designed to equip participants with the skills needed to build **modern, scalable, and data-driven web applications**.

This three-day intensive program is tailored for:

* Software developers
* Computer science students
* Fresh graduates and aspiring full-stack developers

Participants will gain practical experience through real-world projects, guided exercises, and team-based development under the leadership of experienced industry professionals.

### **Workshop Purpose**

At SomNOG, we recognize the vital role of full-stack development in driving **digital innovation and transformation**. This workshop aims to:

* Strengthen hands-on development skills
* Bridge the gap between academic learning and industry needs
* Prepare participants for real-world full-stack development challenges

Through a **practice-first learning approach (60% practical, 40% theoretical)**, attendees will leave with both conceptual understanding and applied experience.

---

### **Workshop Description**

The **Full-Stack Development with the MERN Stack** workshop provides immersive training on building end-to-end web applications. Participants will learn to:

* Design and build dynamic user interfaces with React
* Develop secure backend APIs with Node.js and Express
* Work with MongoDB databases
* Connect frontend and backend systems
* Collaborate in teams to build complete production-style projects

---

### **Who Should Attend?**

This workshop is ideal for:

* Aspiring and practicing software developers
* ICT professionals
* University students and fresh graduates

**Basic knowledge of JavaScript and web development is recommended.** Participants are required to bring their own laptops to fully take part in the hands-on exercises and team projects.
